Game plan: flood the board with cheap lifelink/lifegain creatures,
turn every point of life into stats via Ajani's Pridemate and
Twinblade Paladin, and clear blockers with premium white removal.
Helpful Hunter and Inspiring Overseer keep the cards flowing so you
rarely run out of gas. Curve tops at 5 with Lyra as the finisher.

2 Hinterland Sanctifier   — every creature you play = +1 life
3 Ajani's Pridemate       — the payoff; grows every single turn
4 Helpful Hunter          — 2-drop that replaces itself
2 Youthful Valkyrie       — grows with each angel (Giada, Overseer, Dazzling, Serra, Lyra)

Piloting notes
────────────────────────────────────────
- Mulligan hands without a 1- or 2-drop.
- Sequence lifegain AFTER Pridemate/Twinblade are down when possible.
- Valorous Stance protects Pridemate from spot removal — hold it up.
- Giada's counters make even Serra Angel a 5/5+; deploy her early.
